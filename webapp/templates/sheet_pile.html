Cantilever Sheet Pile in Sand

Embedment depth, maximum moment, and required section modulus for a cantilever sheet-pile wall penetrating homogeneous sand, per Das & Sivakugan (2019, 9th SI) §18.4, pp. 758–764, Eq. 18.11–18.22.

Scope of this first build: homogeneous sand profile above and below the dredge line; cantilever (no anchor); optional uniform surcharge on backfill; water table located anywhere within or below the retained height. Clay (§18.6), layered soil, and anchored sheet piles (§18.9–18.16) are deferred to a later build.

References & Methodology

  1. Das, B.M. & Sivakugan, N. (2019). Principles of Foundation Engineering, 9th Edition, SI. Cengage Learning. Ch. 18 — Sheet-Pile Walls: §18.3 Cantilever sheet-pile walls (p. 757); §18.4 Cantilever Sheet Piling Penetrating Sandy Soil (pp. 758–764), Eq. 18.7–18.22 — the closed-form used by this calculator.

  4. USS Steel (1975). Sheet Pile Design Manual. United States Steel Corporation. Catalog of PZ/AZ sections and allowable stresses.
